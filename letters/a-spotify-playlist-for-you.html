A Spotify playlist for you

by Joanne Amarisa
Melbourne, Australia. Sunday, 8 May 2022.

My Dear Friend,

Will you sit with me this Sunday morning?

In lieu of a written letter, today I wanted to send you a playlist. Modern-day mixtape, if you will. It's not a lot, but I'm sensing a collective need among all of us to breathe. Like we have all been running this race for too long, whatever it is.

So have a seat, Friend, take a breath.

Hope this playlist keeps you company. ❤️

Sincerely,
Jo
 

PS: Tell me your coffee order, or suggest me a song.
