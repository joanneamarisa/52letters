20. Postcard from Tassie

by Joanne Amarisa

Hobart, Australia. Sunday, 4 September 2022.

My Dear Friend,

Scenic places don’t always make our troubles go away, but it does briefly ease our worries, and isn’t that better than what we get most days?

It’s been a while since I last brought my Fuji out for a stroll - and being in Tasmania for a sunny weekend was a prime occasion.

It's sort of Wes Anderson meets Downton Abbey (I say, as someone who's never watched Downton Abbey.) I suppose I should let the photos do the talking. If you ever find yourself on the southernmost tip of Australia in spring time, I highly recommend a visit. :)


Question: What's a place you visited that eased your troubles away?
