14. Ten songs to pick you up

by Joanne Amarisa

Surabaya, Indonesia. Tuesday, 12 July 2022.

Dear Friend,

What's your favourite type of music? We're all subject to our own taste, but because this playlist is mine... I think it's impeccable.

So anyway :) Today I'm sharing with you a list of my go-to feel-good songs which I play literally every day. I start work at 7 AM nowadays, so I always need either a triumphant musical or a trumpet-y jazz band or the bridge of Taylor Swift's All Too Well (10 Minute Version) (Taylor's Version), to get me going. 

They're also useful for dragging myself out of the late-afternoon lull. So consider these when you need a good pick-me-up this work week, or the week after that, or the week after that. Pro tip: Just remember to keep it at enough volume that you don't miss any Microsoft Teams calls. I unfortunately learn from experience.

1. Coldplay: NPR Music Tiny Desk Concert 
(YouTube)
Dare I say I probably listen to this every single day. Chris Martin and Jonny Buckland sings with the For Love Choir Cry Cry Cry, Viva La Vida, Broken, Champion of the World, and — my favourite — 1999 (Prince Cover).

2. FREEDOM by Jon Batiste 
(Spotify / YouTube)
Grammy award-winning Jon Batiste "captures," as one YouTube commenter said, "the true essence of the New Orleans city". 

3. Broke by Samm Henshaw 
(Spotify)
My ultimate getting ready song! Also great for road trips! (I have nothing against Five Guys!)

4. Olivia Dean: Reason to Stay (Live at the Jazz Cafe)
(Spotify / YouTube)
Olivia Dean doesn't merely pass the vibe check, but defines the vibe check in this live rendition of her single, Reason to Stay at the Jazz Cafe in London.

5. PJ Morton's Piano Album
(Spotify)
If you're after something less upbeat and more gentle head-swaying, I can't think of anything more swoon-worthy than this album. I personally listen to Mountains and Molehills and How Deep is Your Love, religiously.

6. Shall Not Want by Maverick City Music
(Spotify)
Speaking of religiously (aha) this was in the top 5 of my 'Spotify Wrapped's for a while. A highly dance-able track for when you want to worship the worries away.

7. All Too Well (10 Minute Version) (Taylor's Version)
(Spotify / YouTube)
I know this doesn't categorise as "feel-good" but honestly... Just between us did the love affair maim you too?

8. Hercules Muses Medley
(YouTube)
Back to upbeat, baby! No time to wallow on this hype train. I'm so obsessed with this medley I think I know all its words by heart. It's the vocals for me! The instrumentals! The sass! All of it!

9. In the Heights
(Spotify / YouTube)
There's something about following Usnavi through the corners of his block, opening his store at the crack of dawn that gets me ready for my day to start with his, too. (If this fails, may I also suggest Hamilton?)

10. Bandung by Yura Yunita
(Spotify / YouTube)
Ending on a sweet, Indonesian note :) Yura Yunita's Bandung blends traditional Sundanese music with delicious jazzy riffs to pay tribute to her (and my mother's) hometown.
 

What gets you going in the morning?

PS: Don't forget to submit a response to our anonymous readership survey — help me write in a way that resonates with you better!

I wish you a serendipitous week.
